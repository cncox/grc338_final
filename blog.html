The Cal Poly swim team had a weekend get away to the beautiful Cuesta Ridge, overlooking all the 9 sister mountains in San Luis Obispo.

Competing at the East LA College Pool, the Cal Poly women placed 6th and the men placed 5th overall at their end of season Conference meet.

Over the month of Decemeber the team trained hard but still managed to have fun, visiting the local Santa and carrying on many team traditions.

The Cal Poly swim team battled UCSB that came down to the final relays. Always a fun meet when rivaleries are present.
